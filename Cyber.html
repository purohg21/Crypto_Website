What is a Capture The Flag Challenge?
A Capture the Flag (specifically in cybersecurity) is usually a competition where individuals can learn and hone their cyberseccurity skills, learning about useful hacking mechanisms and helpful defenses when it comes to code vulnerabilites and finding passwords within a level.

This CTF I've designed for you is not meant to be a competition (it certainly isn't as difficult as any real CTF you may encounter in the future) but rather is meant to serve as a learning tool for the REAL beginner (not a ~novice~ programmer who has a lot more experience than the average person) to cybersecurity and fields such as cryptography and steganography, which you will see appear throughout the CTF.

You can try to approach this linearly (level 0 to level 1, level 1 to level 2, etc.) or skip around if you wish! Each level requires you to look for an answer and gives you clues to help you find it.
