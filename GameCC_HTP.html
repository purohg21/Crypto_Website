How Do I Play This Game?
This game features three different ciphers: Caesar Cipher, Vigenere Cipher, and Atbash Cipher.

You have the option to either encyrpt or decrypt a message in each of these ciphers.

The goal is to solve these without the help of online sources (you could really just look at the page source if you want to cheat anyway, so why not actually try?)

Google is your friend if you're stuck :)
